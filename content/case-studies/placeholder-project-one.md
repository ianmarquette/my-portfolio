## Problem

New users were dropping off during onboarding because the setup flow required too many steps before seeing value.

## Process

We ran five usability sessions and mapped the full onboarding journey. Key pain points: users were asked to configure integrations before they could see any data, which created a blank-state problem on day one.

We tested three alternative flows — a quick-start mode, a template library, and a guided tour — before landing on a progressive disclosure approach that deferred advanced configuration until after the first "aha moment."

## Outcomes

Time-to-value dropped from 14 days to 3 days. 30-day retention improved by 22%.
